About me

Timothy Bertrand is currently pursuing his Masters in Theological Studies at Boston College. His research interests include integrating spirituality and psychotherapy, and social justice regarding gender and sexuality. Timothy received a B.A. in Law from China in 2012. After his graduation, he have traveled to Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Singapore as a visiting scholar. Timothy was previously a Graduate Assistant at Thea Bowman AHANA Center at BC and is proficient in information technology systems and software maintenance. He is preparing for the exams of Comptia A+ which is a professional IT certification from Computing Technology Industry Association. Timothy is working at Center for Teaching Excellent at BC, his company's website is CTE-TimothyXiao.
